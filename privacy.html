Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

This page explains what information we collect, why, and who handles it.

What we collect

If you subscribe to the newsletter on this site, we collect your email address. If you email to apply for coaching, we collect what you send us.

We do not collect payment information, phone numbers, or any sensitive personal data.

Why we collect it

Solely to send you the newsletter, which you consent to when you subscribe, and to reply to you if you reach out about coaching.

Who handles your data

We use third-party services to run the site:

  • Substack – delivers the newsletter. If you subscribe, your email is stored on Substack's servers in the US. Substack privacy policy ↗

We also use Google Fonts to load typefaces on this site. Google may log your IP address when fonts are requested. Google privacy policy ↗

Cookies

This site only sets cookies if you use the newsletter sign-up form embedded on the page (powered by Substack). We ask for your consent before loading that form. If you decline, the form won't load and no cookies are set. You can still subscribe directly on Substack ↗.

How long we keep your data

We keep your information for as long as you're subscribed to the newsletter. If you'd like to be removed, just email us and we'll take care of it.

Your rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete your information at any time. If you're in the EU or UK, you also have the right to object to how we use your data or to request we restrict it.

To exercise any of these rights, email hi@orienting.lu and we'll respond within 30 days.

Questions?

Email hi@orienting.lu and I'll reply personally.
